Order of Saints

Abstract

An online cooperative fantasy themed arena survival game where players take on roles in the "Order of Saints", defending mankind from an endless onslaught of demons that have been summoned into the world.

High Level Concept

Players will create or join existing rooms that can be set to public or private to allow play with strangers and/or friends. Once in a room, players will choose from several playable characters with different abilities and combat styles. The host will select a map, that will be a single scrolling background image, and will have the power to start the game.

Once the game has launched, players will spawn together in the middle of the map, and waves of enemies will commence. Players will have to strategically use their abilities and work together to survive for as many rounds as they can. When a player dies they will have to wait to be revived, or leave the game. When all players have died they will be returned to the character selection screen.

Story

Human sin has finally undone the world. Temptation has lead to the summoning of demons under the false pretense that they could be controlled for fulfilling human desire. Pride, envy, greed, lust wrath, gluttony, sloth...one order has renounced all of these traits and resisted temptation. They are The Order of Saints, and they have been blessed with the power to repel the demon's onslaught and redeem humanity. They are the only hope, the last line of defense.

Mechanics and Controls

  • Menu Navigation and Selection - Mouse
  • Movement - WASD and Arrow Keys
  • Basic Attack - Mouse
  • Powers - QE and Spacebar

Assets

Bare Minimum

The first goal will be to have one playable character that all players will use with one power, one map, and one enemy type. Basic functionality will be the priority before implementing things like private vs public room creation.
